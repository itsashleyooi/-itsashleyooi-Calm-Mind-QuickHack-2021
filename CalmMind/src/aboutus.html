ABOUT US

Our Story

We are a team of four students from Monash University Malaysia. We believe that we could help reduce mental health among university students by creating an interactive platform where students are able to express their thoughts and feelings.

Our aim is to reduce the number of cases of mental health issues among university students and build a community where students are able to interact despite the restriction of on-campus activities.

Why are we doing this?

Due to the current pandemic situation, more people are suffering from mental health illnesses. Even before the pandemic, about 30% of Malaysia's population suffered from anxiety and/or depression. 1 in 5 adolescents are depressed, 2 in 5 adolescents are anxious and 1 in 10 adolescents are stressed.

According to the statistics, it can be said that mental health is an issue in Malaysia. Therefore, we aim to reduce the figures of people suffering from mental health.

What We Offer?

Wordcloud

Wordcloud allow students to see the recent keywords that are entered by other students. The size of the keyword depends on the number of times entered by students. The most entered keyword will be the largest size visa versa.

Forum

A forum page allows students to discuss the theme of the week. It is opened for all students. Students can also use the hashtag feature to tag their pictures.
